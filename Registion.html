Poster Presentation:

1.This event is designing a poster or presenting a paper on technical topics.

4.Poster can be made using pencil, crayon, watercolor, oil paint, etc.
5.Photographs, wires, other 3D objects, and print-outs are acceptable.
6.It must be original and hand made.
7.The Topic of the poster and theme should be clearly mentioned.
8.Posters must be based on Technical topics.
9.The topic is given on spot.
10.Everyone must bring their things on their own. Only charts are provided
11.No internet is provided
